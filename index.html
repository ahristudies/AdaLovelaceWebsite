About me

I'm Ada Lovelace, I'm a mathematician very passionate about creating algorithms. Some people call me the first programmer in history, but I still don't know what that is. How is my job? I create algorithms for your machines, you can see in the testimonials, my most recent work was for a friend of mine Charles Babbage. I really enjoy writing and I have a set of published notes, the name is Notes, yes I am very creative. Well, of course I can't help but mention that I'm Lord Byron's daughter, I love that guy.

My mother has always been very supportive of my work (I think it has something to do with my father, but I prefer not to ask.) and I can proudly say that I come from a family of intellectuals and strong women. I'm currently married (sorry guys!) and I have three kids. Because I was very supportive of creativity, at age 12 I created a book called flyology, maybe that shaped who I am today, who knows?
